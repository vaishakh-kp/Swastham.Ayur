Swastham Ayurveda, the reason for being

Swastham Ayurveda is an accomplished destination for Authentic Ayurveda, serving regional and international clients. Our pursuit is to seek the true meaning of 'SwaStham' the sanskrit word which means 'stay in the self' implying Health.

We give importance to the idea that the Body and Mind exist in web of relations with nature and other co existing factors. The interplay of these being realised as pleasure or pain, in other words health or Illness. We treat patients with complaints, that may range from an aching joint or an illness with serious consequences on life or a disturbance of mind. We work as Family Health Adviser, providing the Holistic care.

Our methods are derived from the classical text books of Ayurveda and Kerala traditional knowledge, which are enriched by the expertise of masters. The activities be summarised in four categories, Chikitsa (Therapeutic Techniques) Oushadham (Therapeutic resources) Sambhasha (Healing Conversations and interactions) Bodhanam (Awareness of life, its challenges and Remedies).

We have great pleasure to recommend Swastham for all.

Participation in your Healing Process

Though we all look identical with common attributes, in the true nature, all are diverse. This is so obvious when dealing our responses to the Disease or Stress.

The success of any remedial measure depends on how accurately we are able to decipher this uniqueness and bring the components together, the most suited to that particular individual. Foundation of Swastham approach is laid on this universal idea of Appropriateness, established by our science. We follow a course of Internal and External regimen as advocatedh by Ayurveda.

The days you spent here will engage you with Panchakarma, other Therapies and Medications. Recommendations for Life style correction with emphasis on Good Eating Habits etc.are elaborated.

You are assisted in all the matters by our trained team and helped to participate in the event of healing and encouraged to adapt it to the life as much as possible. Apart from being corrective the interventions help to alleviate the sufferings.

Scenic location with Fresh Air, Water,Soil and Space in a country side of Kerala, Do nothing moments and journey to your inner Self, all will help you to relax and recharge. We promise to engage you as you are in a holiday during the course of treatments, keeping in mind, every step count to help strike a balance, that is the Wellness.

For any query on your problems or illness please feel free always to have a word with us and we will be most happy to help you.
