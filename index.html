Swastham Ayurveda, the reason for being

Swastham Ayurveda is an accomplished destination for Authentic Ayurveda, serving regional and international clients. Our pursuit is to seek the true meaning of ‘SwaStham,’ the sanskrit word which means ‘stay in the self,’ implying Health.

We give importance to the idea that the Body and Mind exist in the web of relations with nature and other co-existing factors. It is the interplay of these that is realised as pleasure or pain, in other words health or Illness. We treat patients with complaints that may range from an aching joint or an illness with serious consequences on life or a disturbance of mind. We work as Family Health Advisers, providing Holistic care.

Our methods are derived from the classical text books of Ayurveda as well as the traditional knowledge of Kerala, and are enriched by the expertise of masters. The treatment regime can be summarised in four categories - Chikitsa (Therapeutic Techniques), Oushadham (Therapeutic resources), Sambhasha (Healing Conversations and interactions) and Bodhanam (Awareness of life, its challenges and Remedies).

We have great pleasure to recommend Swastham for all.

Inviting you to participate in your Healing Process

Though as human beings we all appear to be the same, the fact is that each individual is unique. And has his/ her own traits that are different from the other. This is so obvious when dealing our responses to the Disease or Stress.

The success of any remedial measure depends on how accurately we are able to decipher this uniqueness and bring together the components most suited to that particular individual. The foundation of Swastham approach is laid on this universal idea of Appropriateness, established by our science. We follow a course of Internal and External regimen as advocated by Ayurveda.

Your treatment regime here would include the renowned Panchakarma, apart from other prescribed Therapies and Medications. Recommendations for Life style correction with emphasis on good eating habits etc. are elaborated.

You will be assisted in all matters by our trained team, and would be helped to participate in the healing process and encouraged to adapt it to your life as much as possible. Apart from being corrective, the interventions help to alleviate sufferings.

The Swastham campus is located in a very scenic spot in rural Kerala, with ample fresh air, pure water and unpolluted soil. All you need do is just sit back and relax, and journey to your inner self, to emerge refreshed and rejuvenated. We promise to keep you in a relaxed vacation mode during the course of the treatments, keeping in mind that every step counts in the journey towards the goal, that is, complete Wellness.

To know more, and for any query regarding your problems or illness please feel free to talk to us and we would be ever happy to help you.
