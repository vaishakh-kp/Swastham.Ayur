Swastham Ayurveda

Swastham Ayurveda is the meeting place where ancient Ayurveda science, nature and people meets for wellness of all.

Our methods are derived from the classical text books of Ayurveda and Kerala traditional wisdom, which are enriched by expertise drawn by the respectful masters in the field. Our methods be summarised in four categories Chikitsa (Therapeutic Techniques) Oushadham (Therapeutic resources) Sambhasha
(Healing Conversations and interactions) Bodhanam (Awareness of life, its challenges and Remedies).

We have emerged as a destination for numerous patients with problems, ranging from an aching joint or an illness with serious consequences on life or a disturbing stress born discomforts of body or mind. We enjoy the privilege of being the family health adviser in many instances. We recommend Swastham for all.
